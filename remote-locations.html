Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any age restrictions for participating in the tours?

There are no specific age restrictions for participating in the tours, but certain activities like snorkeling may have age requirements for safety reasons.

Is transportation provided to and from the tour departure point?

Transportation to and from the tour departure point is not included in the tour packages. However, we can provide recommendations for transportation options upon request.

Is there a refund policy if I need to cancel my booking?

Our cancellation policy allows for flexible cancellations with a full refund if you need to cancel your booking for any reason. Please refer to our terms and conditions for more information.

Is snorkeling included in the tour packages?

Yes, snorkeling is included in some of our tour packages. You can explore the vibrant marine life and coral reefs surrounding Shell Island with our expert guides.

Can I bring food or beverages on the tour?

You're welcome to bring food and beverages on the tour, but please be mindful of any waste and dispose of it properly to help preserve the island's natural beauty.
